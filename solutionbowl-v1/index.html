FAQ

The questions everyone asks
before they book.

Is the UI/UX design really free? +
Yes — completely. UI/UX design is included in every app development engagement at no additional charge. We're able to offer this because our design and engineering teams work under the same roof. There's no third-party design vendor to pay.
What's the typical project timeline? +
Most MVPs take 8–12 weeks from kickoff to App Store submission. Complex apps with integrations or custom backends may take 14–18 weeks. We give you a firm timeline after the discovery call — not a range we inflate later.
Which platforms do you build for? +
iOS and Android, always together. We build cross-platform with React Native or Flutter by default — one codebase, both platforms, native performance. If your project requires native Swift or Kotlin, we handle that too.
How is the project priced? +
Fixed scope, fixed price. We agree on deliverables before we start, and you pay in milestones — not hourly. No surprise invoices. No scope creep unless you request it. We'll give you a detailed quote after the discovery call, not a vague estimate.
What happens after the app launches? +
Every project includes 60 days of post-launch support — bug fixes, crash monitoring, and minor improvements. After that, we offer affordable retainer plans for ongoing development and feature expansion.
Do I need a technical background to work with you? +
Not at all. Most of our clients are non-technical founders and product managers. We translate your business requirements into technical specs — you focus on the product, we handle the engineering. Weekly video updates keep you in the loop without requiring technical knowledge.
